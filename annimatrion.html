ADVANCED DIPLOMA IN HARDWARE AND NETWORKING - ADHN

The ADVANCED DIPLOMA IN HARDWARE AND NETWORKING sets you up
for a great career in fixing computers and setting up networks. This course
focuses on hands-on learning, teaching you all about computers, the
internet, basic electronics, and how hardware and networks work. If you
want to be a computer hardware or network engineer, you need to be
creative, curious, and good at paying attention to details. It's also
important to be able to work well with others in a team
